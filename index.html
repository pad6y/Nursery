A Little About Us

Welcome to Newcastle Daycare, situated in the beautiful surroundings of Elswick park. Our setting is a fully inclusive home from home, where all children are made to feel welcome. We allow the children freedom to play, explore and be themselves.


At Newcastle Daycare we pride ourselves on the quality of care, which we provide to all of the children who attend our setting. We know quality of care is the most important factor for parents and carers when selecting a day care provision. Our highly qualified staff strive to ensure that all children feel happy, safe and secure. Safety and welfare are our top priority.


Our outdoor area holds access to Elswick park, creating a peaceful and stimulating environment for the children to explore and investigate. There is an abundance of resources on offer which caters for all of the children’s different needs and interests.


We encourage parents and carers to be interested and involved in your child’s day at nursery. The use of a secure online learning journey allows us to record and track your child’s development. This ensures parents/carers have access to observations, photographs and a contact with your child’s key worker.


Being Unique

Unique settings

Situated on Elswick park, Newcastle Daycare offers outdoor lead play in beautiful surroundings, which create a peaceful and stimulating environment for the children to explore and investigate. Our purpose built provision is designed to enable your child to independently access all areas promoting their self confidence, learning and development.

Dedicated and highly qualified staff

All of our staff at Newcastle Daycare are highly qualified with many years of experience, or are working towards their qualifications with hands on work based experience. Many of our staff team are parents themselves which adds a strong element of empathy and understanding towards our children and their families. All of our staff team have been recruited via the safer recruitment scheme with relevant DBS (Disclosure and barring service) checks.

Curriculum

We follow the national curriculum, The Early Years Foundation Stage, which sets the standards for the learning, development and care of your child from birth to 5 years old.

Outings

At Newcastle Daycare we engage the children in regular outings, for example a walk to our local supermarket where the children are encouraged to choose their own healthy snacks and resources they may wish to play with. We also provide opportunities for the children to attend outings further afield, for example an outing to the farm, where the children learn about animals and agriculture, giving them a better understanding of the world through first hand experiences.

Local community

The children at Newcastle Daycare will work closely with our local community, engaging in fun and exciting charitable events throughout the year. We also have contact with a local care home where the children will engage in activities alongside senior residents. This interaction of juniors and seniors holds many benefits for example; boosting self confidence, increase in communication and language, learning new skills, and giving all parties a sense of purpose.

Healthy meals and snacks

Healthy meals and snacks are prepared on site by our staff who are all trained in food hygiene. We can work with you should your child have any special dietary requirements, giving that we are a fully inclusive setting. Please notify us on enrollment should your child have any special dietary requirements.
